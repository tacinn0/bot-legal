Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord verification bot owned and operated by Bikelife: Netherlands may collect, process, store, and protect limited information.

1. Ownership and Purpose

This bot is owned and operated by Bikelife: Netherlands. The bot is used exclusively for verification purposes between Discord and Roblox within the Bikelife: Netherlands community.

The bot is not intended for unrelated advertising, profiling, analytics beyond operational necessity, or any purpose unrelated to verification and community access management.

2. Information We May Process

The bot may process limited information required for verification and technical operation, including:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server IDs
  • Roblox user IDs or other Roblox account identifiers used during verification
  • Verification state or status needed to confirm successful account linking

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to:

  • Verify that a Discord account belongs to the correct Roblox account
  • Grant, deny, or manage access based on verification status
  • Maintain bot functionality, reliability, and abuse prevention
  • Resolve verification-related support issues when necessary

4. Data Sharing

Data processed by the bot is not sold, rented, or intentionally disclosed to unrelated third parties. Information is only used internally for the operation of the verification service for Bikelife: Netherlands.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to support verification, maintain community functionality, prevent abuse, or comply with legitimate operational needs.

6. Data Removal Requests

Users may request removal of data associated with their verification record by contacting the administrators or operators of Bikelife: Netherlands. Requests will be reviewed and handled within a reasonable timeframe, subject to operational or moderation requirements.

7. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect stored data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No system, however, can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

This service is not intentionally directed at collecting additional personal information from children beyond what is strictly required for the verification process on supported platforms.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or technical changes. Continued use of the bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For questions, concerns, or removal requests relating to this Privacy Policy, users should contact the Bikelife: Netherlands administrators through the official community channels.
