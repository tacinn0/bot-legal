Verification

This bot is owned and operated by Bikelife: Netherlands.

The bot is used exclusively for verifying Roblox accounts with Discord accounts inside the Bikelife: Netherlands community.

How to Verify

To verify your account:

  1. Join the Discord server
  2. Use the command /verify
  3. Follow the instructions provided by the bot
  4. Complete the Roblox verification steps

Once finished, your Discord account will be linked to your Roblox account.
